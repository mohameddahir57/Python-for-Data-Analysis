## The Challenge: E-Commerce Analytics Dashboard

You've been hired as a data analyst for an online retail company. Your task is to create a comprehensive visualization report analyzing their sales data.

## Dataset Overview
The dataset contains:
- **Order Date**: When the order was placed
- **Category**: Product category (Electronics, Clothing, Home, Books, Sports)
- **Region**: Sales region (North, South, East, West)
- **Sales Amount**: Revenue from the sale
- **Quantity**: Number of items sold
- **Customer Age**: Age of the customer
- **Customer Type**: New or Returning customer

## Your Tasks

### 1. Time Series Analysis
Create visualizations to answer:
- What is the overall sales trend over time?
- Are there any seasonal patterns?
- Which months perform best?

**Recommended**: Line chart with proper formatting

### 2. Category Performance
Analyze and visualize:
- Which product categories generate the most revenue?
- How do categories compare in terms of average order value?

**Recommended**: Bar chart (sorted) or horizontal bar chart

### 3. Regional Analysis
Explore:
- How do sales vary across regions?
- Which regions have the highest and lowest performance?
- Are there regional preferences for certain categories?

**Recommended**: Bar chart, grouped bar chart, or stacked bar chart

### 4. Customer Demographics
Investigate:
- What is the age distribution of customers?
- Do different age groups prefer different product categories?
- How does spending vary by age?

**Recommended**: Histogram, box plot, or violin plot

### 5. Customer Behavior
Analyze:
- What's the split between new and returning customers?
- Do returning customers spend more than new customers?

**Recommended**: Pie chart, bar chart with comparison

### 6. Relationships
Discover:
- Is there a relationship between quantity and sales amount?
- Does customer age correlate with spending?

**Recommended**: Scatter plot with regression line

### 7. Comprehensive Dashboard
Create a final dashboard that combines:
- 4-6 key visualizations
- Clear titles and labels
- Professional color scheme
- Logical layout
